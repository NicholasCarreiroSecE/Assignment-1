Top 3 Games I have Played

Game Title Game Rating Game Description
The Witcher 3 Wild Hunt 4.7/10 The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an acclaimed open-world action RPG that follows Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter navigating a richly detailed fantasy world filled with magic, political intrigue, and moral complexity. Set in the war-torn continent of The Witcher universe, players engage in a captivating narrative where choices significantly impact the story. The game features a vast landscape to explore, challenging combat mechanics, and a deep character progression system. With stunning graphics, immersive storytelling, and engaging side quests, The Witcher 3 offers a remarkable gaming experience that has captivated players worldwide.
Red Dead Redemption 2 4.5/5 Red Dead Redemption 2 is an epic open-world action-adventure game set in the late 1800s, following the story of Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang. As the gang faces the decline of the Wild West, players navigate a richly detailed world filled with stunning landscapes, complex characters, and gripping narratives about loyalty, survival, and morality. The game combines immersive storytelling with realistic gameplay elements, including hunting, crafting, and dynamic interactions with NPCs. With its breathtaking visuals and deep emotional resonance, Red Dead Redemption 2 is a masterful exploration of the American frontier and the struggles of its inhabitants.
Elden Ring 4.4/10 Elden Ring is an expansive action RPG that invites players into a richly crafted open world filled with mystery, danger, and breathtaking landscapes. Set in a hauntingly beautiful universe created in collaboration with George R.R. Martin, the game features a vast interconnected environment teeming with formidable enemies, intricate lore, and deep exploration. Players assume the role of a customizable character known as the Tarnished, embarking on a quest to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord. With challenging combat, strategic gameplay, and a wealth of secrets to uncover, Elden Ring offers an unforgettable experience that rewards both skill and curiosity in its sprawling, immersive world.
